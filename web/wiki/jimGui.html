JimGUI

A guest login page inside some of our kiosk application that stores orders information. Guest can save and load their orders when using our kiosk to build their products at any of the menard stores.

Difference between JimGUI and MCOM is just the look and feel, but they both use the same functions.

What about it

  • It uses Sockets connections to interact with our internal databases
  • JimGUI is used in some of our apps at the start screen and at the end of the applications for saving and loading orders
