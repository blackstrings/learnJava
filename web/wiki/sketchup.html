Short Cuts
Multiple Copies Select obj > hold ctrl > drag move obj to destination > let go > type "*5" to get 5 copies
Locking axis bar use arrow key to lock direction you want
Move by precise unit select move tool > click once on obj > move obj in direction you want > type "2'" to move obj 2 feet
Drawing a shape precisely draw a rectangle > type in "4', 5'" you'll get 4x5 > pull tool > pull up > type 6'
Mirroring objects 3 ways, use plugin Mirror.rb, use scale tool type in -1, right click flip by axis (you can't define a plane though)
Extrude face P > Select a face and click drag
Follow Me Tool draw a diagonal line at corner of cube > follow me > click drag around edge
Fill Hole in outline Line tool > draw a line between any 2 segments
Rotate object Press "M" > hover mouse over the + symbol > click once > drag > dial in degree
Align to or from center of obj M > hover mouse over any edge center and slowly move toward the center of object
Move by vertex don't select anything > M > click drag on vertex (must not be in component mode)
Offset lines press O > click on any lines > drag
