True Sight

An online site used with detecting SLA performance. When we get an EAM about SLA, the assigned person of the week who is to take care of the SLA EAM tickets, will drill down to true sight to help them debug where the SLA issue is occurring in what app. True sight is one of the tools we use, but Dynatrace is another more useful tool.

Please refere to the SLA for instructions for the time being.
