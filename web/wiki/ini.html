INI Files

INI extension files are used by many of our C++ applications. INI files are consider a poor man's way of storing data. It is like an old version of XML. We store product data information inside INI files and call them from the apps when we need to.

Highlights

  • Majority of the time, when updating the ini files we will only add/remove products
  • The garage app has the largest product selection and therefore a large SKU list. Therefore, the SKU are borken into smaller separate ini files.
  • We usually map product SKU numbers to Scodes inside ini files
  • We hope to eventually phase out from using INI in our new web app conversions.
